Regions

Greyreach

A sprawling city shrouded in an eerie mist and is the grand centre of all life in Farwen, serving as the capital, it serves as the homeplace for the king.

Northshore

The oldest known civilisation on Farwen, Northshore is home to the Varkir people. Built into a cove, it is a pretty coastal city that prides itself on seafaring tradition.

Orastead

A grand city claimed to be formed by magic itself. Beautiful, intricate architecture lines the streets and is home to the Orastead Academy.

Burcliff

A fortified town perched atop sea cliffs, famous for its lighthouse and storm-watching festivals.
