Anah'diel

Overview

Anah'diel, also known in the common tongue as Snow Elves, are an aloof people who often reside in small communities in the snowy peaks of Farwen. Though, many have migrated to and reside in regions like Orastead, Farewood and Greyreach where they have become semi-populous.

History

The Varkir are an ancient people, with the city of Northshore established long before the capital of Greyreach. Varkir spent many years cultivating the land, shaping it to their will, and because of this are treat with respect and appreciation by most other races even if the Varkir are an aloof community. In History, they were known for conducting many great wars and fought battles against travellers who came to Farwen to colonise the land for their own.

Physical Appearance

Anah'diel are known for their androgynous beauty, with equal parts delicate and sharp features, long platinum hair and pale skin with some bearing glowing markings on the flesh. In height, they are often somewhat tall, but are no taller than 7.5 feet at their highest. Their eyes are often grey or blue, and they have pointed ears decorated with jewels and ornaments. Their clothing is often made from the finer materials, with a preference for furs and leathers, and they are known to wear intricate jewelry which is handmade and often passed down through generations.
