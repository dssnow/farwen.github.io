Human

Overview

Rumored to be descendants of the ancient Varkir, humans are the most populous race on Farwen. They are an adaptable race with a wide range of cultures and traditions, usually passed down from family to family and traditions can vary greatly depending on where they are from. Humans are known to be ambitious, often seeking to expand their territories and influence. They are a resilient race of people and are incredibly resourceful.

History

The Varkir are an ancient people, with the city of Northshore established long before the capital of Greyreach. Varkir spent many years cultivating the land, shaping it to their will, and because of this are treat with respect and appreciation by most other races even if the Varkir are an aloof community. In History, they were known for conducting many great wars and fought battles against travellers who came to Farwen to colonise the land for their own.

Physical Appearance

Humans, in race, are very diverse in appearance. They can have a wide range of skin tones, hair colors, and eye colors. Their height can vary significantly, with some being quite tall and others shorter. Humans are known for their adaptability, which is reflected in their physical traits.
