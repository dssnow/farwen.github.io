Tal'diel

Overview

The Tal'diel, or Mountain Elves, are a proud race of elves who are known for their intense connections to nature and ability to become some of the most prosperous druids in Farwen. They are usually found in Farewood, where they have built their homes in the trees. They are usually very peaceful, though the more rugged warrior-aligned Tal'diel can be intense and intimidating. They are quite solitary but are not cold to outsiders, in fact they are welcoming and enjoy learning about other cultures and welcome visitors to their lands.

History

The Tal'diel are often suggested to be half-breeds of both the Varkir and the Snow Elves, Anah'diel. They are a people who have lived in the mountains near Cragvale and Farewood for many centuries.

Culture & Traditions

  • Rite of the Iron Path (coming-of-age)

Physical Appearance

Varkir are a kind of giantkin, tall people standing between 7.5 feet tall to 11 feet tall at their highest. They have a robust build, with broad shoulders and muscular limbs. Adult women are generally just as muscular as men, with wide chests and impressive strength of the arms. Their skin tones range from pale to deep brown, often adorned with tribal tattoos that tell the story of their clan and personal achievements. Their hair is typically dark, though blond is not unheard of, and they often wear it long and braided.
