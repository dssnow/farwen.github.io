Overview

Greyreach is a sprawling city nestled between misty mountains and ancient forests. Known for its towering stone walls and bustling markets, it serves as a hub of trade and culture in the region.

History

Founded centuries ago by mountain clans, Greyreach has weathered wars, plagues, and political intrigue. Its resilience is legendary among neighboring realms.

Culture

Greyreach is famous for its annual Moonfire Festival and its diverse population of artisans, merchants, and scholars.

Geography

The city is surrounded by the Whispering Woods to the east and the Ironspine Mountains to the west, making it both a natural fortress and a crossroads for travelers.
